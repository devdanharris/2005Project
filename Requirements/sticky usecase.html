User Story

As a moderator, I've noticed the same questions get posted very frequently. I need to be able to make frequently asked questions easily findable, as well as remove duplicate posts.

Use Case

Title:

Sticky Posts with High-Demand Information

Primary Actor:

Moderator

Scope:

Moderators and users of the SUD

Stakeholders and Interests:

    Moderator - Wants to avoid having multiple posts on the same topic
    Users - Want to be able to quickly find important information without searching through posts

Precondition:

Moderator is logged in and has a post to sticky

Minimal Guarantee:

A stickied post appears at the top of the post order

Success Guarantee:

The post is marked as a sticky, appears as visually distinct, and shows up at the top of the list of posts for users

Main Success Scenario:

    1) Moderator finds a post to sticky
    2) Moderator selects "sticky" from the moderator options provided
    3) The list of posts is re-ordered with the sticky post staying at the top
    4) Users see the sticky in a visually distinct manner at the top of the post order when they refresh

Extensions:

    a) User is not a moderator:
      a1) Non-moderators will not be shown the option to sticky posts
    b) The user deletes their stickied post:
      b1) The stickied post will be removed as normal, and the reserved spot at the top of the post order will be released.
    c) Multiple posts are marked as sticky:
      c1) The n stickied posts will reserve the top n spots in the post order, sorted from most to least recently stickied.
